Flat Iron Steak

Best Flat iron steak submitted Cocina JNOTS

Ingredients

  • Flat Iron Steak
  • Olive Oil
  • Minced garlic cloves
  • Chopped parsley
  • Chopped rosemary
  • Chopped chives
  • Dry red wine
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Dry Mustard powder

Steps

  1. Stir olive oil, garlic, parsley, rosemary, chives, redwin, salt, pepper and mustard powder together
  2. Place steak and mixture into a sealable bag to marinate for 2-3 hours
  3. Heat skillet on medium-high heat and sear and cook the steak for 3-4 minutes each side
  4. Remove the steak and let it rest for 5 minutes prior to serving
